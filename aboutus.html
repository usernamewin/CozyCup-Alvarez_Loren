CozyCup Coffee House began as a dream to create a cozy haven for coffee lovers. Our commitment to quality and community sets us apart.

Meet the Team

Owner: Ruby Jane 'Bean Boss' Kim
The visionary behind the cafe, Ruby is fueled by passion and espresso. When she's not dreaming up new menu items, she's perfecting the art of the perfect cup of coffee. Her motto? "Caffeine is my creativity's best friend!"

Manager: Rose Anne 'Café Captain' Park
The calm in the storm and the organizer of chaos. Rose makes sure everything runs smoothly with a smile, and her favorite tool? A perfectly brewed latte to fuel her efficiency!

Barista: Kai Enrico 'Espresso Alchemist' Licauco
Crafting the perfect cup is an art for Kai. Whether it’s a cappuccino or a macchiato, he’s always stirring up something special and complete with a side of charm

Baker: Lucas Rafael 'Pastry Magician' Palma
Master of dough and all things sweet, Lucas is the wizard behind the oven. From croissants to cakes, if it’s fresh and delicious, it’s his creation. Flour power, indeed!

Cashier: Gian Kyle 'Master of Transactions & Smiles' Velasco
With a friendly smile and lightning fast hands, Gian makes sure your orders are processed and you’re on your way. He believes every payment should come with a sprinkle of good vibes!

Busser: Jameson Dien 'Ambassador of Clean Vibes' Lim
The clean up crew with a can-do attitude! Jameson is always on his toes, keeping the cafe sparkling while delivering good vibes all around. He’s the unsung hero behind every smooth service!
